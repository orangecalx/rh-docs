BUILD NOTES


  • Before powering on for the first time, check for shorts between the GND, +12, and -12 test points.

  • Values for all components are listed on the board itself.

  • The microphone itself will rest on the resistors a bit, this is normal!

  • The components marked OUT and CV are 3.5mm audio jacks - they're marked as such for easy troubleshooting.
